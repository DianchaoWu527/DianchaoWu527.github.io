  • ISmART Research Project: Random Matrix Theory and Quantum Expanders
    Iowa State University
    Spring 2026 – Present
    Exploring random matrix models arising from Haar-distributed unitary matrices, with a focus on malnormal matrices, commutator lower bounds, and non-normal spectral behavior. This work connects probabilistic matrix models with quantum expanders and uses numerical simulations to investigate asymptotic conjectures in finite-dimensional operator theory.
